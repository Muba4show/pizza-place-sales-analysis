# 🍕 Pizza Place Sales Analysis

## Project Overview

This project presents an exploratory data analysis (EDA) of one year's sales data from a fictitious pizza restaurant. The objective of the analysis is to uncover meaningful business insights from customer orders, sales transactions, and menu performance using Python and its data analysis libraries.

The dataset consists of four related CSV files:

* **Orders** – Contains the order ID, date, and time of each customer order.
* **Order Details** – Contains the quantity and pizza ordered for each order.
* **Pizzas** – Contains the pizza size and selling price.
* **Pizza Types** – Contains the pizza names, categories, and ingredients.

These datasets were merged into a single DataFrame to simplify the analysis and answer the business questions provided in the assignment.

---

## Project Objectives

The primary objectives of this project were to:

* Calculate the total revenue generated from pizza sales.
* Determine the total quantity of pizzas sold.
* Find the total number of customer orders.
* Identify the number of pizza types available on the menu.
* Calculate the average selling price of pizzas.
* Determine the busiest hours of the day based on customer orders.
* Analyze total sales for each day of the week.
* Identify the top five bestselling pizzas.
* Examine monthly sales trends throughout the year.
* Identify pizzas that are underperforming on the menu.
* Perform additional exploratory analysis to gain deeper business insights.

## Data Analysis Process

The analysis followed these major steps:

1. Imported the necessary Python libraries.
2. Loaded the four CSV datasets.
3. Inspected the datasets to understand their structure.
4. Checked for missing values and verified data types.
5. Merged all datasets into a single DataFrame using common keys.
6. Created a new **Revenue** column by multiplying quantity by price.
7. Performed exploratory data analysis (EDA).
8. Created charts and visualizations to communicate findings.
9. Interpreted the results and documented business insights.

## Key Findings

The analysis revealed several important business insights:

* The restaurant generated strong annual revenue from pizza sales.
* Thousands of pizzas were sold across more than twenty thousand customer orders.
* Customer demand peaked during lunch and dinner hours.
* Sales varied across different weekdays and months.
* A small number of pizza varieties contributed significantly to total sales.
* Some pizzas consistently recorded low sales and may require menu review or promotional efforts.
* Larger pizza sizes and certain categories generated higher revenue than others.

These findings can help management make informed decisions regarding staffing, inventory management, marketing strategies, and menu optimization.
